bSafe

The app bSafe is an app where users can select guardians to watch them while they livestream througha video whenever they would like. For example, if a user is walking through a dark alley, the user can choose to livestream as they walk, which will automatically record a video. Additionally, they can turn on an alarm to startle predators or make emergency phone calls. The pros are that this seems to be a very effective way to startle predators, get help, and record all activity. However, this app is not available globally.
